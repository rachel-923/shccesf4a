Chinese Club

學會宗旨:

中文學會乃本校中學學務組轄下的學術組織，成立於2006年，其宗旨是透過舉辦不同的專題課堂和學校活動，以增加學生對中國文化的認識，培養其學習中華文化的興趣，使她們在文史知識、語文能力、品德情操各方面都有所增進。

Science Club

AIM:

The goal of Science Club is to encourage students to put their science knowledge learnt into practice as well as arouse their interest in science through carrying out experiments and activities related to daily life applications. In addition, we aim to serve as a platform to organize various visits for students to explore different specializations in scientific field.

Humanities Club

Club introduction:

The Humanities Club aims at enhancing students’ historic and geographic literacy for the better understanding of our world. With the increased communications between places around the world, the establishment of the HC carries the vital responsibility in guiding students to explore the varieties of cultures and countries by means of history and geography. Through deeper understanding, a positive attitude of students towards multiculturalism and diversity is hoped to be cultivated.

Drama Club

活動宗旨:

話劇社成立之目的在於培養學生對話劇之興趣，為學生提供一個發展才能的機會，激發她們的潛能。此外，本會亦致力透過工作坊培養她們的合作精神和團隊意識，提升她們的自信心和自我表達能力，加強其自理和勇於面對困難的能力。

English Club

AIM:

The English Club aims to promote in its Club and in the school campus the use of English in a relaxed and enjoyable way. It strives to arouse and promote students’ interest and usage in English by organizing diverse English activities and games in which students are guided to utilize structures and language in different circumstances.

Environmental Protection Team

AIM:

EPT aims to promote and deliver messages about environmental issues through competitions and workshops.

The Young Apostles

AIMS:

The Young Apostles is a catholic society set up in 1986. Its members include all of the catholic students of the school. The spirit behind is to live out the gentleness, goodness and sacrificing spirit of Jesus in their daily life. Besides, the Young Apostles serve the Lord by spreading the Good News of God’s unconditional love that He is our loving Father who is always by our side. They take part in organizing religious and formative activities in school and hold weekly meeting.

Broadcasting Team

AIM:

Broadcasting is an art which unfolds dimensional perspective lives through planes.Broadcasting Team is a group of students who are enthusiastic in video-taking and live broadcasting. We record the activities held in school as well as the smiling faces of students. Through these practices, students can have higher abilities in video-taking and live broadcasting. Also we provide opportunities to students in editing video clips.
